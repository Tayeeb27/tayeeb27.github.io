About Me

The world of Computer Science and technology captivates me. My fascination for the field was ignited during my younger years in which I observed the work of a close relative who specialised in program development. Her ability to innovatively solve complex computer science problems intrigued me. Since then, my journey into the world of programming has been a hugely rewarding endeavour. At my time in university, at La Fosse and in my own time, I have developed expertise in a broad range of programming languages and utilised my knowledge to solve practical challenges like creating a COVID Mashup Webpage and a Library Services Website. After La Fosse, I moved into industry as a Junior Full Stack Developer at Kukd.com, contributing to the development and maintenance of websites and applications across Kukd.com, EuroFoods, Masala Bazaar and Bvaah. I have also delivered freelance projects for clients such as TktTiger and FormsOffline, and I am now a Full Stack Engineer at TalentMapper, building full stack features from APIs and data through to responsive interfaces. My love for technology is matched only by my enthusiasm for life's adventures, both personal and professional.
