## Background

In LAP 3 (3rd Module) we had a group project where we had to create a React app beneficial for students. We chose to build a calendar app but what makes it different from the standard is its interchangeable theme and Chrome extension with the ability to share your calendar with others by generating a link.

My role was main Frontend Developer which I enjoyed because it gave me more experience in React.
