## Background

Out of my early projects, this one was by far my favourite. I had to create a booking and ordering system for a restaurant. The project was completed in VB.NET using the waterfall method. I enjoyed it because I had interactions with a real client and the project was fun to complete — I was one year into basic coding and the fact that I could deliver something meaningful was incredibly rewarding.
