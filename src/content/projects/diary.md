## Background

In LAP 2 (2nd Module) at [La Fosse](https://www.lafosse.com/academy) we had a hackathon to create a diary application in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and PostgreSQL where you could create, read, update and delete diary posts, storing everything in a database.
