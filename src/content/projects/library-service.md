## Background

At [La Fosse Academy](https://www.lafosse.com/academy) we were required to solve the problem for The Florin & Burkes County library who are experiencing reduced staffing and potential closure despite increase in demand. To address this, we launched a website where users can make book reservations without staff assistance.

The system consists of a frontend with the ability to reserve and search for books and filter by category, plus a forum page for users to discuss the community or books. The backend uses PostgreSQL to store all data.

## My Role

My role was Project Manager. However, I was mainly involved with the frontend of the application. I also managed the backend team, helping them fix any bugs they came across.
